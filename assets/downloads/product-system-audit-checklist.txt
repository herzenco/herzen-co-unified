Herzen Co. Product System Audit Checklist

Use this to identify the operating gaps that make product work feel reactive.

1. Roadmap clarity
- The next 30 days are tied to business outcomes.
- Every roadmap item has an owner and a reason.
- Tradeoffs are visible, not implied.

2. Backlog quality
- Old ideas are pruned or re-qualified.
- Top items have acceptance criteria.
- Customer signal and founder judgment are both represented.

3. Sprint cadence
- Planning creates decisions, not just a meeting record.
- Reviews produce learning and next actions.
- Risks and blockers are visible before they become surprises.

4. Stakeholder alignment
- Leadership, engineering, design, and vendors share one product thread.
- Decisions are documented where the team can find them.
- Escalation paths are clear.

5. Metrics
- The team knows which delivery, customer, revenue, or operational metric matters now.
- Metrics are reviewed on a cadence.
- Output and outcome are not confused.

6. First 30-day reset
- Pick the three highest-friction gaps.
- Name the owner for each.
- Decide what proof of improvement should exist in 30 days.
